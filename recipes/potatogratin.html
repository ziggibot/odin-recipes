Potato Gratin

Hot and full of cheese for winter evenings

Potato gratin can be done with many different ingredients

This one we are going to make with tasty vegetables

  • Potatos
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables
  1. Boil the potatoes
  2. Cut the Vegetables
  3. Put it all in a baking pan
  4. Put the cheese on top
  5. Bake for 30 Minutes at 200 Celsius
