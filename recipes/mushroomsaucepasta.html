Pasta with mushroom sauce

Saucy and mushroomy

Fresh mushrooms, hot pasta and cheesy sauce ist the best!

Cook it like at your favorite Italian restaurant

  • Mushrooms
  • Linguine Pasta
  • Soft cheese
  1. Clean and cut the mushrooms
  2. Heat them in a pan
  3. Add the cheese to the pan
  4. Cook the Linguine pasta
  5. Serve the pasta with the mushroom sauce
